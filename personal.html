Coaching

I was Head Coach at Edinburgh University Table Tennis Club from 2018 to 2023 — five consecutive annual contracts, running sessions for beginners through to BUCS-level competitors. Coaching taught me how much of the game is in the head, and how quickly someone can progress once they stop fighting the ball.

Credentials:

  • UKCC Level 2 Table Tennis Coach
  • EFA Emergency First Aid certified
  • PVG Protecting Vulnerable Groups (Scotland) cleared
  • PVC Protecting Vulnerable Children certified
